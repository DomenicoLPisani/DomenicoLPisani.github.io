Dreams

About

Since I can remember, upon waking up, I often recall dreams vividly (even complex ones).

However, it wasn't until I was in my early twenties that I discovered (talking to a friend), it's not usual: so, as soon as I wake up, I've developed a habit of sending him messages describing them.

He has encouraged me multiple times to compile the material and make it public: a little bit late, here's the result.
